Compatibility information

Supports UEFI/BIOS systems, removable media (USB, CD/DVD), and ISO formatting. Legacy systems below Vista may require optical media, where PowerShell must be installed as well.

External Components

Includes Rufus UEFI:NTFS drivers, reformatted from FAT12 `.img` to ISO for broader compatibility with modern burning applications.
